MEMBERS CALENDAR ABOUT ZINE LIBRARY PRESS

COMMUNITY ROOM

June 2016

Friday, June 24, 2016

Founded in Richmond, VA in 2014, Community Room is dedicated to creating opportunities for live artists. We actively support multimedia artists, musicians, dancers, performers, presenters, cross disciplinary makers, socially active artists and those who seek to push the genre boundaries of “art”.

Come by the Little Berlin Annex space for an evening of live art consisting of musical acts, videos, presentaions, performance art and more. You won’t want to miss this!

There is an interactive work that involves produce and juicing, please bring produce of any kind if you are interested in participating :) Thanks!
