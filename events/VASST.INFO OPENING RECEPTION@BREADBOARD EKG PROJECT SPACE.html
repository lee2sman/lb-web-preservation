VASST.INFO OPENING RECEPTION@BREADBOARD EKG PROJECT SPACE

April 2011

Friday, April 29, 2011

First opening of the dual site exhibit pairing Breadboard's EKG Project Space with little berlin, VASST.info, Very Amateur Society for Science and Technology. 

VASST.info asks select artists to produce a science experiment in place of the art object. As a result the artists have brought forth a unique insight relating to science and technology.

Artists at Breadboard’s EKG Project Space:

  • Brandon Joyce
  • Daniel Wallace & Derek Frech
  • Jamie Dillon
  • Sam Cusumano
