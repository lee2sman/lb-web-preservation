BREAKING NEWS

October 2009

Friday, October 2, 2009

LITTLE BERLIN, Philadelphia. For the month of October, Little Berlin will host Preston Link and Gabriel Boyce’s Breaking News. The news-themed installation documents and interprets popular (and sometimes, not-so-popular) news stories. Using fine art as a method of reporting, the show delves into the depths of contemporary media.

Join us at the Opening Reception October 2, 6pm-11pm
Show runs through October 31st, 2009
